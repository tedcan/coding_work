AT&T Foresees $4 Billion Charge
09:07 11/24/2011, twocents@thestreet.com (TheStreet Staff), dt, equities, m&a, mobile, regulation, s, top, vz, TheStreet.com

NEW YORK (TheStreet) -- AT&T said Thursday it expects to take a $4 billion pretax charge in the fourth-quarter to reflect breakup fees related to its planned acquisition of T-Mobile USA.

The announcement is an acknowledgment that the prospects look increasingly bleak that the $39 billion transaction passes regulatory muster.

In a news release, AT&T said the $4 billion charge would include $3 billion in cash and $1 billion in spectrum. ...
