Air France-KLM shares rally after broker upgrade
09:04 11/24/2011, MarketWatch.com - MarketPulse

LONDON (MarketWatch) -- Shares of Air France-KLM rallied 9% in Paris trading Thursday and was one of the top gainers in the Stoxx Europe 600 index . Societe Generale upgraded its rating on the airline to hold from sell after the recent selloff in the stock. Air France-KLM's shares have dropped 31% so far this month. The broker warned, however, that "significant uncertainties remain, both macro and company specific, and we believe there remains downside risk to consensus forecasts."
