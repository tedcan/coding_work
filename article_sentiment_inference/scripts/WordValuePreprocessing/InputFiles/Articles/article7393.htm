60/40 Bears to Bulls
13:43 11/22/2011, Bespoke, bespoke market poll, market analysis, Think BIG

Yesterday we asked Bespoke readers which level they thought the Dow would reach first -- 11,000 or 12,000. At the time, the Dow was trading right in between the two levels at 11,500.

As shown below, 59% of the 511 participants said the index is heading down to 11,000, while 41% said it's heading back up to 12,000. At least for now, bearish sentiment rules, which is a bullish sign if you're a contrarian.
