Europe may be falling apart as a solution to the debt crisis grows more elusive each day, but despite the turmoil, fund managers still say there are strong stocks to be found across the Atlantic.

The faltering euro will help exports at technology giant Siemens, says Audrey Kaplan, portfolio manager for the Federated InterContinental Fund. In addition, she says the so-called "German GE" will benefit from the European Central Bank's recent decision to lower interest rates, allowing Siemens to reinvest in its businesses. ...
