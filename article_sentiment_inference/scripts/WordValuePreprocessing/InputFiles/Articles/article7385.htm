16:36 11/23/2011, Bespoke, market analysis, Think BIG

With a decline of 4.43% leading up to tomorrow's holiday, 2011 will go down as the worst Thanksgiving week ever for the S&P 500 (since the government officially designated Thanksgiving as the fourth Thursday of November in 1941).
