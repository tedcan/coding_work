Australian stocks decline in early trading
18:19 11/24/2011, MarketWatch.com - MarketPulse

SYDNEY (MarketWatch)-- Australian shares fell in early trading Friday, with retail and media firms among the worst performers, after U.S. markets were closed for a holiday. The benchmark S&P/ASX 200 shed 0.9% to 4,009.00. The index has traded down for the past five sessions as concerns about Europe's debt crisis and the impact on global growth weighed on sentiment. Retailers extended previous session losses, with department store David Jones Ltd. down 2.5% and Myer Holdings Ltd. off by 3%. Media stocks were mostly weaker, with Fairfax Media Ltd. losing 1.9% and Ten Network Holdings Ltd. down 1.1%.
