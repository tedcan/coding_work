The X Prize Foundation: Now count to a hundred
00:00 11/24/2011, The Economist: Science and technology

We are delighted that you have such good genes
IN BRITAIN, those who live to be 100 years old receive a birthday card from the queen. In the future, centenarians everywhere may also receive a call from a geneticist. If they do, he or she will be seeking a sample of DNA that might, eventually, help to reveal the genetic components of extreme longevity. The more immediate use, however, will be in a competition.
